Where did it all start?

It's actually kind of funny how we all met. Only a few of us personally knew some of the members before the team was formed. The first time we all got to meet each other, even if it was online, was during an MMO game match. You see, like most tech-savvy people on this day and age, we do enjoy playing games with our friends and meeting new people through them. We hit it off soon enough and chose to create a small gaming community on Discord for players from our country.
It didn't take long for the word to spread that many of us on there were IT professionals. The need to constantly exchange ideas and opinions with other IT professionals from our country led us to expand our community's theme to Information Technology related matters. With the passage of time, those of us that played that aforementioned match arranged to meet one day and go for a cup of coffee. While we were discussing various topics, someone brought up ethical hacking and how teams of people were working hard to make sure that any serious exploits were brought to light. After a long discussion, an idea was formed. That idea was what is now known as the "Codestruction Team".
